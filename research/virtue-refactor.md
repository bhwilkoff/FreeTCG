## Proposed Faction Restructure

The agent recommends three faction renames so the cluster names are also action-coded. Original → proposed:

- **Presence** → kept (Showing-Up, Listening, Holding)
- **Connection** → kept (Building, Asking, Thanking)
- **Growth** → **Play** (Goofing, Cheering, Wondering) — *gives joy a faction, not just a card; absorbs Curious-as-wonder*
- **Agency** → **Steadfastness** (Steady, Defending, Choosing) — *more on-theme; "Agency" is academic*
- **Integrity** → **Repair** (Mending, Naming, Trying) — *the dad-action of integrity is mending, not abstract uprightness*
- **Resilience** → kept (Adapting, Tending, Teaching)

| Faction | Virtues | Vibe |
|---|---|---|
| **Presence** | Showing-Up, Listening, Holding | "I'm here, I hear you, I've got you." |
| **Connection** | Building, Asking, Thanking | "Let's make it together. What do you think? I see you." |
| **Play** | Goofing, Cheering, Wondering | "Joy is a faction." |
| **Steadfastness** | Steady, Defending, Choosing | "I won't flinch. I won't let you be hurt. I'm picking this on purpose." |
| **Repair** | Mending, Naming, Trying | "I broke it; I'll say so; I'll try again." |
| **Resilience** | Adapting, Tending, Teaching | "I bend; I show up daily; I pass it on." |

Mapping is intentionally non-rigid — many virtues ladder across two factions. (Listening could live in Connection; Holding could live in Resilience.) The faction is the *primary* color; the virtue is the *card type*. MTG's color-pie / creature-type duality.

- **Mending** — could read sentimental; the action (apology) is concrete enough.
- **Naming** — could sound abstract; rescued by use ("Naming the feeling," "Naming the bias").

**Ratio:** 11 warm-or-neutral / 4 serious (Defending, Mending, Naming, Steady) / 3 playful (Goofing, Cheering, Wondering). Honors the "lead with joy" feedback.

### Joy is not a virtue (structural correction)

In v1 the agent put Goofing and Cheering into the Growth faction. That was a category error. Per Decision 019, joy already has its own structural home:
- The **Highlight Reel** captures Moments — funny beats, warm scenes, surprises
- The **`Play` tag** marks cards that often trigger Moments (~30% of card pool)
- **Year cards** ("The Hot Lava Living Room," "The Surprise Trip") carry joy at the turn level

So the virtue list doesn't need a "joy virtue." The virtues are about *what kind of dad you're being*; the Moments are about *what's about to happen that you'll remember*. Both matter; they live in different layers.

### Tone audit on v2

Reading the 18 aloud:

> Showing-Up. Listening. Holding. Welcoming. Asking. Thanking. Wondering. Trying. Teaching. Choosing. Advocating. Naming. Owning. Mending. Forgiving. Adapting. Tending. Returning.

- 0 traditional-stoic words (Steady, Defending, Goofing all out)
- 0 introspective abstractions (Conscious, Introspective, Unfinished as a tag — out)
- All 18 are progressive-action verbs or verb-participles
- Mix of small-stakes (Tending, Asking, Thanking) and high-stakes (Advocating, Owning, Forgiving)
- Several explicitly capture the In-Progress / growth identity (Trying, Wondering, Returning, Mending, Owning)
- Forgiving + Welcoming + Holding give the kindness/empathy throughline the user asked for

### Risks / open calls

1. **"Teaching"** — could read traditionally if all Teaching cards are "the dad knows, kid learns." Mitigate by including reverse-Teaching cards (the kid teaches the dad — the user's "Equitable" post explicitly: "learning Pokémon from the youngest"). The card pool needs to model both directions.
2. **"Owning"** vs **"Confessing"** vs **"Apologizing"** — Owning is the broadest (responsibility for actions and consequences). Apologizing is too narrow. Confessing has religious overtones. Owning wins.
3. **"Returning"** — could be confused with returning a card to hand mechanically. Card-tag wording will need care so the keyword reads as *the dad coming back* and not *the card moving zones*.
4. **The Growth faction** is the lightest weight (Wondering, Trying, Teaching). If playtest reveals it doesn't pull its weight strategically, consider adding a fourth virtue or absorbing one from another faction. Possible adds: Discovering, Becoming, Practicing.
